About Us

We're a team dedicated to improving life after rectal cancer surgery through technology and care.

Our Mission

iLARS was created to help patients manage Low Anterior Resection Syndrome (LARS) — a common condition after rectal cancer surgery that affects bowel function and quality of life.

Our mission is to provide simple, effective tools for symptom tracking and to connect patients with their care teams for better outcomes.

Who We Are

We are a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, developers, and researchers working together to improve patient outcomes after colorectal surgery.

Our approach combines clinical expertise with user-centered design to create tools that are both medically sound and easy to use.

Our Values

  • Patient-centered: Every decision we make prioritizes patient well-being and quality of life.
  • Evidence-based: Our tools are built on validated clinical measures and current medical guidelines.
  • Privacy-first: We protect patient data with pseudonymous identifiers and secure storage.
  • Accessible: We believe healthcare technology should be available to everyone who needs it.
